Clinical Framework

A Proven, Holistic Treatment Approach

Biopsychosocial-Spiritual Model (BPSS)

We treat the whole person — body, mind, relationships, and purpose. This comprehensive framework ensures that no aspect of your well-being is left unaddressed during recovery.

Minnesota Model

A globally recognized, abstinence-based framework combining clinical care, peer support, and structured recovery principles designed to foster sustained sobriety.

Comprehensive Care Services

Clinical & Therapeutic Programs tailored to your needs.

Liver Function Test (LFT)

Clinical intake blood test to assess liver health, guide safe detox protocols, and inform individualized treatment.

Medical Detoxification

Safe, medically supervised withdrawal management to minimize discomfort and health risks with nursing oversight.

Psychiatric Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation by a psychiatrist to identify and treat co-occurring disorders (Dual Diagnosis).

Residential Rehab

Structured 24/7 live-in program in a supportive, drug-free environment designed for immersive recovery.

Individual Counseling

One-on-one sessions with licensed psychologists to explore triggers, address trauma, and build coping strategies.

Group Therapy

Daily therapeutic groups focusing on relapse prevention, life skills, emotional regulation, and peer support.

Family Therapy

Sessions to heal family relationships, educate loved ones about addiction, and build a supportive home environment.

Psychoeducation & Skills

Workshops on addiction as a disease, healthy routines, vocational skills, and financial literacy.

Holistic Therapies

Physical fitness, nutrition planning, mindfulness, meditation, art therapy, and recreational wellness activities.

Spiritual Care

Faith-based or spiritual pastoral services aligned with beliefs to foster hope, purpose, and inner strength.

Aftercare Planning

Personalized discharge planning with ongoing support options for a sustainable, lifelong recovery.
