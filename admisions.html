Starting Your Journey

Clinical Admission Process

We ensure a smooth, dignified, and supportive transition into our care through a structured 5-step process.

1

Initial Contact

Reach out via phone, WhatsApp, or email for a confidential consultation. Our admissions team is ready to listen and guide you.

2

Pre-Admission Assessment

A qualified clinician conducts a phone or in-person assessment to determine suitability, urgency, and the specific level of care needed.

3

Medical & Psychosocial Evaluation

Upon arrival, a full medical check-up, substance use history, mental health screening, and biopsychosocial assessment are completed.

4

Consent & Orientation

Client and family sign informed consent forms. The client receives a full orientation to the program rules, daily schedule, and expectations.

5

Detoxification

If required, safe stabilization begins immediately under 24/7 medical supervision to ensure comfort and manage withdrawal symptoms.

Program Structure

Treatment Process from Beginning to End

The entire process is individualized based on clinical needs, progress, and ASAM criteria.

Days 1–7

Phase 1: Assessment & Stabilization

Comprehensive evaluation, medical detox (if needed), orientation, and initial goal setting to establish a solid foundation for recovery.

Weeks 1–4+

Phase 2: Intensive Treatment

Daily schedule including group therapy, individual counseling, educational lectures, holistic activities, and 12-Step work. Focus on understanding addiction, identifying triggers, and building coping skills.

Ongoing Residential

Phase 3: Deepening Recovery & Skill Building

Advanced therapy for trauma/mental health, life skills training, vocational planning, family sessions, and relapse prevention planning. Increased responsibility and reflection.

Final 1–2 Weeks

Phase 4: Relapse Prevention & Discharge

Finalizing aftercare plan, linking to community support groups, outpatient options, and alumni network. Gradual reintroduction to external responsibilities where appropriate.

Family Support & Involvement

Addiction affects the entire family, and healing together is a crucial part of long-term success. We guide clients and their loved ones through rebuilding trust, improving communication, and setting healthy boundaries.

  • Family therapy sessions integrated into Phase 3.
  • Psychoeducation on addiction as a disease.
  • Guidance for establishing a supportive home environment.
