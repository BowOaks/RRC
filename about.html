Value Proposition

A Safe, Holistic Environment

Resolutions Recovery Centre offers a safe, structured, and compassionate inpatient rehabilitation program exclusively for male clients struggling with Substance Use Disorders (SUD), Process Addictions and co-occurring mental health conditions. In a serene, home-like environment, we provide evidence-based, holistic treatment that addresses the root causes of addiction, promotes lasting sobriety, and rebuilds a meaningful life.

Our Promise

Sober life, better living.

We don’t just help men stop using substances — we equip them with the tools, skills, and support to achieve long-term recovery, improved mental health, stronger relationships, and a purposeful future.

Key Message At Resolutions Recovery Centre, every man is treated with dignity and respect. Recovery is possible. Through professional clinical care, peer support, family involvement, and a strong spiritual foundation, we guide clients from crisis to transformation, one day at a time.

Standards of Care

Accreditations & Compliance

Resolutions Recovery Centre is fully committed to the highest standards of care and operates in compliance with Kenyan regulations.

NACADA Accreditation

We are accredited by the National Authority for the Campaign Against Alcohol and Drug Abuse (NACADA), the regulatory body for addiction treatment facilities in Kenya.

KMPDC Compliance

Our clinical team adheres to guidelines from the Kenya Medical Practitioners and Dentists Council (KMPDC) for medical and psychiatric care.

Copies of accreditation certificates and licenses are available for verification upon request or during facility tours.
