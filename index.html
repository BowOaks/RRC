The Resolution Way

More Than Sobriety — A Better Way to Live

At Resolutions Recovery Centre, we provide a safe, structured, and supportive environment where men can break free from addiction and rebuild their lives. Clinical Framework

Biopsychosocial-Spiritual Model (BPSS)

We treat the whole person — body, mind, relationships, and purpose. This comprehensive framework ensures that no aspect of your well-being is left unaddressed during recovery.

Minnesota Model

A globally recognized, abstinence-based framework combining clinical care, peer support, and structured recovery principles designed to foster sustained sobriety.
